INTERIOR DESIGN

HAUSPUNKT VISIO is a virtual tool for planning that enables you to visualize your 2D floor plans in 3D. This establishes a new standard in terms of precisions planning your living space.

Building a new house, renovation or a new interior design, HAUSPUNKT VISIO targets architects, builders, houseowners, general contracts, wholesellers, investors, producers and real estate agencies. We enable you to provide your customers with a virtual visit to their dream living space in a Timesaving, Realtime and location independent manner.

Individual Layouts can be changed and visualized in Realtime without technical knowhow. Including a selection slip with all your used materials.

VISIO provides your and your customers with an interactive layout as we included more than 70’000 products – tiles, sanitary equipment, furniture, lamps etc – and also considers dynamic light conditions (Day / Night). A completely new feeling of space.

HAUSPUNKT VISIO increases your efficiency and maximise the possibility to individualize the planning, gives you room for inspiration and supports the imagination of your customers.
